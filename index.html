Our Story

Welcome to Welly Desi Bites – where tradition meets innovation in every handcrafted, eggless delight! As a passionate home-based bakery, we create exquisite cakes, cupcakes, tea-time treats, pastries, and even gluten-free options, tailored to your unique tastes and celebrations.

Each creation is lovingly made to transform any event into a memorable experience or to simply add a touch of sweetness to your day. Discover the art of eggless baking – a delicious, inclusive way to celebrate life’s special moments.
